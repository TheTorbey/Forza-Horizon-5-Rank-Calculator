Description

The Prestige system recognizes experienced players in the Forza Horizon 5 community.
Prestige is first awarded once you've earned enough Global Influence to reach Rank 200.
Throughout Forza Horizon 5, players with Prestige levels are identified by a numbered star next to their Gamertag. When you Prestige, you will revert to Rank 1, and your new Prestige star distinguishes your experience. This is visible to all other players.
The rank level is displayed next to the Gamertag. Together with Prestige it gives information about a player's rank. With each increase in prestige, the number of required ranks also increases.
Menu can also be controlled with keyboard or Xbox controller
